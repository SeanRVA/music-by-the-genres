Music by the Genres

What is Music by the Genres?

Music by the Genres is a learning challenge website. Basically, you're going to go through 4 pages to learn about the Classical, Rock, Pop, and Country genres of music. Want to learn more about this epic test? Read the rules and guidelines ahead! We wish you the best of luck if you decide to take the challenge.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Each page will contain information about a musical genre
  • Important information will be highlighted
  • A quiz for the genre will be available at the end of the page
  • Don't open another tab to cheat on the quiz
  • To pass the quiz, you need to score at least 80%
  • If you fail the quiz, study up and try again
  • Passing a quiz is the only way to move on to the next page/li>
